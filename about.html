About Us

DEVASHRR CLEANING SERVICES
Excellence in Service Delivery

DEVASHRR CLEANING SERVICES is a 100% black female-owned company founded on 14 March 2019. We are based in Gakhoe Village near Dithakong, Northern Cape, and operate in the John Taolo Gaetsewe region. We offer comprehensive quality services in construction, general supply, and cleaning.

  • Our Mission

  • Our Vision

  • Our commitment

To build affordable houses for the needy community as part of social responsibilities. To involve youth, women and disabled people in the development of their potential. To engage staff in decision making of the company so that they can be part of the get wise family. To provide government institution, private sector and individuals with excellence in all the services we are providing.

  • Social Responsibility

  • Youth & Women Empowerment

  • Staff Involvement

  • Excellence in Service

To be a leading empowerment and enriched service provider, offering efficient, effective quality and prompt service to government, private sector and individuals. To add value to the lives of our employees. To engage competent staff that will deliver excellent and quality standard of service to clients.

DEVASHRR CLEANING SERVICES started on 14 MARCH 2019. This private company is based at the village of Gakhoe near Dithakong (Northern Cape). Miss Dithebe is the sole shareholder and founder. This company is 100% black owned by a female who is a previously disadvantaged individual. We are committed to provide excellent service, which is mutually beneficial to our clients and to Devashrr Cleaning Services.
